# Campaign Overview

**Owl's Nest** is a technical and progression-focused Cobblemon campaign.

Players advance through a fixed trainer sequence featuring:

- Rival battles
- Gym Leader-style encounters
- Team Arroba battles
- Elite 4 fights
- Champion battle

Each trainer is configured through Radical Cobblemon Trainers systems.

## Design Philosophy

The campaign is designed around:

- Competitive mechanics in a PvE environment
- Clear difficulty scaling
- Thematic team identities
- Strategic doubles battles
- Progression gates based on trainer defeats
